Letter of Information and Informed Consent
We are delighted to invite you to participant in the online research study outlined below. Your
participation is entirely voluntary, and you can withdraw from the study at any time.
Purpose of the experiment:
The purpose of the experiment is to learn more about how people process complex visual information
from natural environment. You will be fully debriefed on the rationale underlying this experiment
upon completion.
What you will be doing:
You will make judgements based on the stimuli or answer questions, as instructed to you on the
computer screen.
Time commitment & compensation:
This experiment takes approximately 35-40 minutes to complete. You will receive course credit for
your participation.
Risk & Benefits:
There are no risks to you participating in this experiment. Your biggest benefit will be knowledge. At
the end of the experiment, we’ll explain all of the ideas that went into making this study and provide
some additional readings related to the study. If you’re interested, let us know and we will send you a
copy of what we write about this experiment when we’re done. Send us an email at
(bwlab.experiments@gmail.com) for more information.
Confidentiality, Participation and Withdrawal:
No one will know that you participated in this experiment and the data that you produce from this
experiment will never have your name attached to it. Everything that happens in this experiment will
stay completely confidential. Ultimately, we aim to present the results of this experiment at
conferences and publish them in a journal, magazine, or newspaper. If we do, you will never be
mentioned. Should you decide to withdraw from the experiment, you will still receive course credit.
Contact:
If you have any questions about this experiment, you may email us at
bwlab.experiments@gmail.com. If you have questions about your rights as a participant, you may
contact the Office of Research Ethics at ethics.review@utoronto.ca or 416-946-3273. Please note that
you may also save a copy of this consent form for future reference.
